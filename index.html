About Us

Vision

To make Ras Al Khaimah a leading city in outdoor comfort, environmental quality, and sustainable urban development.

Mission

To study outdoor environmental conditions and provide solutions that improve comfort, reduce heat stress, and support better public spaces.

Our Team

Our team includes researchers, engineers, planners, and students working together to develop practical solutions for urban comfort.

Services

Urban Heat Mapping

Creating and analyzing heat maps to understand temperature patterns in Ras Al Khaimah.

Outdoor Comfort Studies

Studying outdoor spaces to improve thermal comfort for residents and visitors.

GIS and Data Analysis

Using GIS tools and environmental data to support planning and decision-making.
