About Us

Dedicated to discovering practical, scientific pathways for improving livability and thermal comfort across vulnerable hot desert urban areas.

Our Vision

To establish the RAK Center for Outdoor Comfort as an authoritative baseline hub across the GCC region for microclimate intelligence, steering urban development frameworks toward optimal ecological compatibility and thermal safety.

Our Mission

To systematically measure, map, and mitigate urban heat island intensities throughout Ras Al Khaimah through modern GIS infrastructure, empirical data modeling, community surveys, and actionable design handbooks.

Our Services

We collaborate closely with government bodies, private real estate developers, and academic partners to provide premium technical consulting.

Urban Heat Mapping

Utilizing state-of-the-art thermal satellite tracking and land surface temperatures to highlight precise neighborhood vulnerability matrices.

Outdoor Thermal Comfort Studies

Conducting in-situ field measurement metrics (PET, UTCI calculations) to calibrate architectural spatial structures to human metabolic needs.

GIS & Spatial Analysis

Developing robust multilayered geographical maps highlighting relationships between building densities, materials, and localized cooling vectors.

Environmental Monitoring

Installing micro-weather station grids to track real-time changes across humidity, solar radiation indices, and convective current changes.

Design Advisory & Guidelines

Formulating rigorous code compliance frameworks detailing ideal cool roof applications, building height ratios, and orientation strategies.

Micro-Climate Management

Optimizing wind flow corridors and ventilation aerodynamics across urban canyons to prevent dangerous heat pockets within the city grid.

Climate Change Adaptation

Preparing city infrastructure for long-term extreme heat trajectories via predictive computational forecasting across decades.

Urban Heat Island Reduction

Engineering customized targeted solutions focusing on reflective albedo changes and high-efficiency evaporative urban setups.

Education & Research

Conducting training bootcamps for practicing municipal engineers, alongside comprehensive technical publication structures.
